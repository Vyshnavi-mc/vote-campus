About the E-Voting System

This platform allows students to register, nominate candidates, check their nomination status, view the list of eligible voters, and cast their votes securely online. The system is designed to ensure a transparent, efficient, and easy voting process for all participants. Your participation is crucial for the democratic process within the student body.
